01
Data
Raw material
Your business already generates everything it needs to know.
Every transaction, booking, order, login, invoice, and interaction is data. It exists — scattered across your POS, your accounting software, your CRM, your e-commerce platform. Orgmind connects all of it, from any source, without requiring your IT team (or having one at all).

02
Information
Structure & meaning
Orgmind learns what your data actually means.
Raw records become structured, queryable information. Orgmind builds a semantic understanding of your specific business — mapping every column, every relationship, every domain term — so when you ask a question in plain English, it knows exactly where to look and what the answer means in your context.
What Orgmind understands
Products have margin, velocity, and stock levels. Customers have lifetime value and repeat-purchase likelihood. Campaigns connect to revenue and discount depth. All of it linked, all of it queryable.
03
Knowledge
Patterns & relationships
Patterns your spreadsheet would never find.
Orgmind doesn't just retrieve data — it reasons over it. It generates hypotheses, runs evidence-driven analysis across your entire data landscape, and surfaces the patterns that matter: correlations, trends, anomalies, and relationships that no dashboard would ever show you unprompted.
Orgmind discovers
Oat Flat White sells 3.4× faster on Fridays. Current stock: 4.2 days. Supplier lead time: 3 days. A weekend stockout is highly probable unless action is taken by Thursday.
04
Wisdom
Judgement & context
What this means — for your business, right now.
Knowledge becomes wisdom when it's evaluated against your specific context: your margins, your thresholds, your risk tolerance, your history. Orgmind's critic layer weighs the evidence, checks for confounders, and produces a grounded judgement — not just a data point, but a recommendation with reasoning attached.
Orgmind's judgement
Stockout this weekend would cost an estimated £840 in lost revenue and affect your highest-rated menu item. The Friday pattern has held across 6 of the last 6 weeks. Confidence in this signal: high.
05
Action
Where others stop, Orgmind closes the loop
The step every other platform skips.
Most tools stop at wisdom — they show you a chart and leave the rest to you. Orgmind closes the loop by automating what can be automated and surfacing what needs your decision. When a threshold is crossed, a rule fires, and something actually happens: a reorder is placed, a task is created, an alert is sent, a workflow is triggered. Each action comes with visible reasoning, evidence, trigger conditions, and auditability, so control stays with your team.
Action fired — 06:14 this morning
Reorder sent to Roasters Direct — 20kg Oat Blend, express delivery
Triggered because: stock below 5-day threshold AND Friday velocity pattern detected AND weekend revenue at risk (est. £840). Delivery confirmed for Thursday.
